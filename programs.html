• Co-ordination between corporates, if shared facility
• Staff & personal matters

Child care
24/7 care (day & night care)
At Krio, we provide waddlers & toddlers’ unrestricted access to
synchronized self-learning.
We offer :-
• Prudently designed stimulating activity zone
• Pampering & safe play area
• learning program with a focus on holistic development
• ECCE trained tutors
• Nutritionist certified sensory based nutrition
Our facility is monitored 24/7 and is
CCTV equipped with live streaming
portal access to parents.

(13 months to 2.6 years)
At krio, we take extra care to address to the hygiene,
learning & dietary (post nursing from mother) needs of infants.
We offer :-
• Nurturing environment
• Comfortable and quiet ‘infant nap rooms’ with
private nursing area.
• Specially trained care-givers
• Unique infant learning program
• ECCE trained gentle tutors
• Nutritionist certified Infant diet plans
“Our Infant care program is designed to enable nursing mothers
to restart their career post maternity leave “

Corporate Child care
Onsite Childcare – professionally governed exclusive child care facility,
within the premises of the corporate, KRIO offers assistance in...

• Setting up the facility including Floor plan & safety aspects
• All Staff and Personnel Matters
• Training, Monitoring and evaluation
• Glitch-free Day-to-Day operations, activity implementation
• 100% compliance to statutory requirements of the corporate

As a service partner of the corporate, we remain committed towards
facilitating the corporate to attain it’s talent goals.

• Less employee absenteeism
• Presenting child care as a part of employee welfare program
• USP during recruitment
• Stress-free environment for working mothers
• Reduces attrition of employees
